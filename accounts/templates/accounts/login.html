Trust AI Lab Platform for STEM Education

The Trust AI Lab platform, developed by the ICCS-IMU team, is an Exploratory Learning Environment designed to enhance students’ inquiry and problem-solving skills through virtual and remote labs.

The Trust AI Lab platform consists of the following components:

  • Plato, an AI Learning Companion for STEM education: Supports students by providing automated guidance and feedback in STEM laboratory courses. It also monitors student engagement and provides personalized learning recommendations.
  • Trust AI Lab Authoring Tool: Helps teachers design and implement inquiry-based learning scenarios.
  • Dashboard Analytics: Assists teachers in assessing students’ performance.
