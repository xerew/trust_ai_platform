Discovery Space

Discovery Space is an EU-funded Erasmus+ Partnerships for Innovation - Forward Looking project that aspires to develop a roadmap for the AI-Enhanced Classroom for Deeper Learning in STEM that facilitates the transformation of the traditional classroom to an innovative environment that promotes the scientific exploration and supports the development of key skills for all students.

Fostering Artificial Intelligence Trust for Humans towards the optimization of trustworthiness through large-scale pilots in critical domains

FAITH aims to provide the practitioners and stakeholders of AI systems not only with a comprehensive analysis of the foundations of AI trustworthiness but also with an operational playbook for how to assess, and build trustworthy AI systems and how to continuously measure their trustworthiness.
